### Reality check on "200 sites"
Sites #123–200 in most such lists on the internet are either (a) exact duplicates under different names, (b) dead/inactive domains, or (c) low-quality lead-gen sites that resell your data. I'd strongly recommend **automating against the 🟢/🟡 ~90 sites above** rather than trying to force all 200 — quality of signal matters more than site count for your Sheet.

If you *specifically* want me to pad this list to 200 with smaller/regional/niche boards anyway, say so and I will — just flagging the trade-off first.
